Iceberg Lake, Montana

Take a bike tour up to Iceberg Lake. This is one of the most beautiful bodies of water located in the Glacier National Park. The trail is about 9.6 miles long and follows a scenic dirt trail. There is usually moderate traffic on this trail during the summer.

Green Gates Trails, Montana

Located in the West Glacier of Montana, there is a great and fairly easy tour for a 10 mile trail. This trail is used for both mountain bikers and hikers. The trail is very green and beautiful all along the way and at the end of the trail there is a gorgeous fresh-water lake.
